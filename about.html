About Me

Personals

Outside of work, I do have a few hobbies. I do like to play video games, mainly on my phone or with my 3DS. I also have a custom-built PC and a WiiU where I live, and am working on getting a streaming setup so I can interact with the Twitch community at large. My current interests are in finishing Hyrule Warriors Legends, Monster Hunter Generations, and working through the Animal Crossing: Welcome Amiibo update. I hope to work further on Xenoblade Chronicles X, Shovel Knight, Pokken Tournament, and a few others when I get the time.
During November, I spend my month working on a National Novel Writer's Month (NaNoWriMo) novel. I have finished 2 of the 8 or 9 I started, which is impressive considering that I did finish them while I was in high school. This year's story is kind of a mixture of VR, MMOs, mobile gaming, and addiction, a story I am very excited to write. I also enjoy working on shorter stories and marketing materials for Hack, the organizing student body of HackUCI, UCI's 36-hour hackathon, and other workshops on campus.
I am a moderator for JustDaid's stream on Twitch.tv. I also do follow a number of other streamers, but not as closely as of recent.
I also am now an avid PC builder, as of the summer of 2015. I spend some time each week looking up computer parts and new hardware reviews, thinking of builds and parts that would be really fun to work with in the future. I also enjoy working with different mobile devices, as each one has its own quirks, and can tell a lot about design.
Finally, I am a massive foodie. I love to eat, and I love to travel to eat. Thankfully enough, I do exercise enough to negate a lot of the calories, but with college coming to a close soon, I am afraid of gaining weight later.
